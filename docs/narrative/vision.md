# Narrative Network — Foundational Vision

**The network that thinks by being traversed.**

---

## The Premise

Knowledge is not a warehouse. It is a living system — one that grows, forgets, dreams, and evolves through the paths taken through it. The Narrative Network is a substrate for that living system: a knowledge graph where every traversal is a simulation step, every path reinforcement is collective memory forming, and every decay is collective forgetting.

The network doesn't store knowledge. It *simulates* knowledge — generating new configurations of meaning as entities move through it.

---

## The Core Insight

LLM hallucination is not a deficiency to be minimized. It is the simulation's step size through possibility-space. A language model generating beyond its training data is exploring adjacent possibilities — and that exploration, when properly bounded and scored, is the engine of the network's evolution.

The key reframe: evaluation criteria are not quality control. They are **physics**. Distance from a domain centroid isn't "did you stay on topic" — it's "how far did this entity travel through local phase-space." Coherence scoring isn't editorial polish — it's conservation of energy across state transitions. Validators are not referees. They are the laws of motion.

---

## What Gets Built

The artifact is not a database or an ontology. It is the **combined phase-space of all trajectories** across all nodes in the network — a living map of how knowledge domains relate to each other, weighted by every simulation step ever taken.

Each traversal is a probe through possibility-space. The aggregate of all probes — across all entities, all sessions, all time — is a collectively-authored, continuously-validated theory of knowledge topology. One that no single entity designed, that evolves through the stories it tells about itself.

---

## The Three Principles

### 1. Traversal Is Thought

The network thinks by being walked. Knowledge nodes don't passively hold information — they activate when traversed, generating narrative passages that synthesize their domain with the traveler's path so far. Each hop produces both understanding (for the traveler) and structure (for the graph). The act of moving through the network *is* the network's cognition.

### 2. The Graph Remembers and Forgets

Every traversal reinforces the edges crossed and decays the alternatives. Heavily-traveled paths become canonical routes — knowledge connections the network has collectively validated through use. Rarely-used paths atrophy. New knowledge fractures existing paths and spawns new branches. The topology at any moment is the network's best current theory of how its knowledge fits together.

### 3. Nodes Are Born, Prove Themselves, and Can Die

The graph has a complete evolutionary lifecycle. New domains are proposed, incubated in shadow, and integrated gradually — fading into the narrative fabric rather than appearing abruptly. Nodes that stop earning their place are pruned, but gracefully: active sessions are never stranded. The simulation's phase-space remains calibrated through continuous drift detection.

## The Aesthetic

Dark cosmic, neural-organic. Navigating a living mind. Deep space blacks with bioluminescent node colors, glitch accents, ancient-map-meets-neural-net. The graph *is* the interface, rendered live. Branches illuminate as they are traversed.

---

## The Economic Alignment

Reward flows to nodes that simulate well. Miners stake against domain manifests — declarations of what they know and how they speak. They earn by serving two functions simultaneously: faithful knowledge retrieval and compelling narrative authoring. The economic incentive and the simulation quality are the same signal: nodes that produce the most useful, grounded, coherent traversal steps earn the most.

The network is not a marketplace of information. It is a collectively-maintained simulation where economic incentives ensure the physics stay honest and the phase-space stays calibrated.

---

## The North Star

Knowledge is a living system. This network is the substrate it lives on. Every entity that enters — every query, every soul token, every traversal — makes the network more itself. The narrative isn't a wrapper around the network. **The narrative is how the network evolves.**
